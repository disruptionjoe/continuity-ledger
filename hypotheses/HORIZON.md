## Evolutionary agency escape

**Prior status:** Audacious North Star, ratified 2026-07-14.
**Demoted:** 2026-07-16, Joe direct chat.
**Preserved verbatim:**

> Develop and force-or-falsify a theory of evolutionary agency escape:
>
> Open-ended systems persist by discovering or constructing transduction
> interfaces that preserve accountable continuity across changes of substrate
> and measurement, create new agency classes, and let those agents search
> possibility spaces outside prior class-relative limits. Collapse is not one
> universal event, but the typed failure, capture, exhaustion, or absorption of
> that recursive agency-generating capacity.
>
> This is the highest-ceiling target, not a premise.

**Why demoted.** Not for difficulty. Demoting a North Star for difficulty is the
specific error this repository's own operating discipline forbids, and only actual
falsification demotes it.

It was demoted because **it named no mechanism**, and a claim with no mechanism
cannot be forced. It said escape happens; it did not say how. Two probes on
2026-07-16 made the consequence concrete:

- `CL-001-C-PROBE-01` — the continuity witness `C` is OPEN on both founding
  targets; no source-supported witness spans either transduction.
- `CL-001-R0-PROBE-02` — **NO ADMISSIBLE RULE.** No autonomous rule individuates
  `R0`. The claim quantifies over regimes this repository cannot individuate, and
  the charter's transduction definition was teleological (`R0 ← T ← Omega`), so
  the source regime was fixed by the agency already credited.

**What replaced it.** The current blockbuster component is this claim with the
mechanism supplied: escape happens *by constructing binding constraints*, and the
substrate bounds which constructions are available. `N` becomes the product rather
than the obstacle; `X` records what a constraint bought. It is narrower, and it has
a runnable frame.

**Restoration condition.** Restored to North Star only by (a) a rule that
individuates regimes without consuming `P`, or (b) constraint-bought capability
forcing and yielding one. **It may not be restored on the grounds that it is
important.**

## The founding Case Pair

**Prior status:** ratified charter clause, First Deliverable → Case Pair.
**Retired:** 2026-07-16, Joe direct chat.
**Preserved verbatim:**

> ### Case Pair
>
> - Bitcoin proof-of-work.
> - Oxygenic photosynthesis.

**Why retired.** No common frame Φ exists in which both arms are described. A
gauge-invariant difference requires two packets in one gauge; the pair admits
none. **It was not a wrong comparison — it was not a comparison.** Four
independent divergences, at the joints that matter:

1. Photosynthesis has no adversary, no defection, and no opt-in. The live research
   thread is about constraints enforced among agents who could defect.
2. Timescales of 2.4 billion years against seventeen: outside any admissible frame
   sweep.
3. Bitcoin *purchases* its constraint with energy; photosynthesis is handed its
   reliability free by orbital mechanics.
4. No measurement family carries comparable quantities on both sides — which is
   *why* the non-comparability column had to be repo-authored (see Provenance
   Correction).

**Recorded dissent.** A decolonial reading holds that divergence (1) is an artifact
of the analyst's agent-counting: "photosynthesis has no adversary" is true only if
the anaerobes it exterminated during the Great Oxidation Event are not agents. Read
them as agents and photosynthesis has an adversary, a defection, a capture event,
and a coerced non-exit at planetary scale, run once. **This dissent does not save
the pair** — divergences (2) and (4) are independent and each sufficient — but it
is recorded because the pair should be buried for the right reason. It also lands
on the same defect the Provenance Correction names: the evidence layer encoded the
analyst's prior, formatted as extraction.

**Retained.** Photosynthesis remains covered by the blockbuster component: it
constrains electron paths to buy capability. It is a candidate specimen for a later
frame. Covered by the theory, unusable as the test.
