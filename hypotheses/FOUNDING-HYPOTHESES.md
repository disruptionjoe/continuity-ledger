# Founding Hypothesis Portfolio

Status: proposed and falsifiable; no hypothesis has passed.

## CL-H1: Typed Ledger Existence

**Statement:** A nontrivial subset of regime-crossing systems admits a common
typed ledger for continuity, loss, imports, agency, no-go escape, and feedback
without identifying unlike quantities.

**Model class:** Material, biological, computational, economic, and
institutional transductions with observable source and target regimes.

**Positive witness:** Not yet constructed. The prior Bitcoin proof-of-work /
photosynthesis witness was retired with the prior CL-001. The active candidate
witness must come from the dollar / Bitcoin Interval Sweep at a declared frame.

**Strongest null:** Ordinary causal process description with no additional
discrimination.

**Falsification:** The schema either becomes vacuous enough to fit every causal
chain or fragments into unrelated domain schemas with no shared constraints.

## CL-H2: Agency-Surface Discriminator

**Statement:** Durable agency-generating transductions contain an identifiable
surface of sufficiently stable expectations or affordances that distinguishes
them from energy/material conversion alone.

**Model class:** Systems that create, recruit, or differentiate adaptive actors
or processes.

**Positive witness:** The target cases pass a preregistered agency test while
matched conversion-only controls fail.

**Strongest null:** Existing agents simply exploit an output; no agency class
or action space is generated.

**Falsification:** Controls and targets cannot be separated without subjective
or retrospective labels.

## CL-H3: Class-Change Escape

**Statement:** Some apparent escapes from no-goes are constructive class
changes that preserve the original no-go while making a new action space
available.

**Model class:** Processes with a named class `K0`, explicit obstruction, and a
constructible transition to `K1`.

**Positive witness:** A packet identifies the exact changed assumption or
interface and demonstrates a capability unavailable in `K0`.

**Strongest absorber:** Optimization, randomization, or hidden oracle access
already allowed inside `K0`.

**Falsification:** Alleged escapes violate the no-go assumptions, import the
answer without accounting, or remain ordinary search inside `K0`.

## CL-H4: Recursive Agency Feedback

**Statement:** In genuinely open-ended cases, generated agency changes the
future transduction, measurement, or incentive regime rather than merely
consuming a fixed output.

**Model class:** Multi-stage systems with downstream adaptive action and
observable feedback.

**Positive witness:** A documented second- or third-order change creates new
interfaces, agents, measurements, or action spaces.

**Strongest null:** Stable control loop, path dependence, or growth in a fixed
state space.

**Falsification:** Downstream effects do not alter future regime structure or
are fully captured by a fixed transition system.

## CL-H5: Non-Scalar Composition

**Statement:** Continuity ledgers can compose as typed relations even when no
universal scalar value is conserved across their stages.

**Model class:** Sequential transactions with compatible boundary types.

**Positive witness:** A composition rule preserves provenance, losses, imports,
and escape obligations and rejects incompatible joins.

**Strongest null:** Post hoc narrative concatenation.

**Falsification:** Composition requires arbitrary conversion factors, loses the
load-bearing domain fields, or accepts incompatible stages.

## CL-H6: Agentic Discovery Advantage

**Statement:** Governed divergent-agent search followed by typed packetization
and absorber pressure produces cross-domain hypotheses with better rejection,
boundary, and falsification quality than unstructured thematic synthesis.

**Model class:** Repeated research runs with fixed source budgets and scored
outputs.

**Positive witness:** Blind reviewers can distinguish the governed pipeline's
artifacts by preregistered rigor criteria.

**Strongest null:** More text and persona variety create only synthetic novelty.

**Falsification:** The method offers no reproducible gain after controlling for
source coverage and reviewer effort.
