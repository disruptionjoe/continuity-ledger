# Example Roster

Status: v0 roster
Date: 2026-07-12

## Selection Rule

The ten examples were selected to maximize divergence while preserving some possibility of rigorous accounting.

They include:

- formal obstruction;
- distributed no-go;
- computational open-endedness;
- AI model collapse;
- ecological regime shift;
- institutional anti-collapse;
- operational resilience;
- physiological overload;
- cultural/linguistic source loss;
- construction-history measurement.
