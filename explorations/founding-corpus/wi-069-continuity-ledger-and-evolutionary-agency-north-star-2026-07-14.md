## The Realistic Hypothesis

### Continuity Ledger Hypothesis

> Open-ended systems require accountable continuity across transductive transactions: as energy, value, material, legitimacy, information, or capability moves across substrates and measurement regimes, something must remain traceable enough for agency to use it, while loss, dissipation, externality, capture, and conversion must also be accounted for.

This is realistic because it does not require a universal scalar called "emergence" or "value."

It asks for a ledger:

```text
source substrate / quantity
  -> transduction interface
  -> target accounting regime
  -> agency-enabled action
  -> created value / capability / residue / loss
```

## The Wild North Star

### Evolutionary Agency-Escape Hypothesis

> Where continuity ledgers allow agency-enabling quantities to survive substrate and measurement transitions, evolutionary pressure favors systems that discover new transduction interfaces, create new agency classes, and search new possibility spaces around local minima and class-relative no-goes.

This is the audacious claim.

It says:

```text
continuity across transduction
  -> stable enough surface for agency
  -> goal-oriented search
  -> innovation / new solution classes
  -> escape from prior local minima or no-go class
```

## Why The Bitcoin Case Matters

Bitcoin is not merely:

```text
electricity -> Bitcoin
```

It is:

```text
electromagnetic energy / hardware / time
  -> proof-of-work
  -> chainwork and settlement security
  -> hard monetary policy / value surface
  -> human incentive
  -> mining, holding, infrastructure, development, regulation, entrepreneurship
  -> new monetary and technological possibility space
```

The continuity question:

```text
What persists from electricity expenditure into chainwork,
from chainwork into monetary value,
from monetary value into human action,
from human action into new capability?
```

The continuity is not perfect conservation.

There are losses:

- heat;
- hardware depreciation;
- fees;
- spreads;
- regulatory friction;
- failed investments;
- externalities;
- speculative self-reference.

But the system creates an accountable path through which physical expenditure becomes a policy/value surface that attracts agency.

## Physical Analogy: Dissipation And Work

The analogy to heat dissipation is useful but must be handled carefully.

In physics:

```text
energy is conserved, but usable work can degrade into heat
```

In transductive value systems:

```text
agency-enabling potential may be transported, degraded, amplified, captured, or dissipated
```

The ledger is not saying monetary value obeys energy conservation.

It is saying:

> The important scientific object may be the accounting continuity of agency-enabling potential as it crosses regimes.

## Initial Hypotheses

### CL-H1: Continuity Ledger Hypothesis

Agency-generating transductions require an accountable continuity relation across substrate and measurement transitions.

Falsification:

Strong agency-generating examples work without any identifiable continuity relation.

### CL-H2: Dissipation / Leakage Hypothesis

Collapse often occurs when agency-enabling potential leaks, dissipates, or becomes unaccountable during regime transition.

Falsification:

Collapse cases show no relation to loss, leakage, untracked externality, broken provenance, or accounting mismatch.

### CL-H3: Agency Surface Hypothesis

The continuity relation matters because it creates a stable enough surface for goal-oriented agents to plan around.

Falsification:

Agents reliably coordinate and innovate without any stable predictability, policy, reward, or orientation surface.

### CL-H4: Local-Minimum Escape Hypothesis

New agency surfaces allow search outside the local minima of the prior class.

Falsification:

The generated agency only optimizes inside the old class and never opens a distinct possibility space.

### CL-H5: Class-Relative No-Go Escape Hypothesis

Transductive agency does not necessarily violate a no-go theorem; it changes substrate, measurement class, or agency class so the old no-go no longer governs the same search process.

Falsification:

All apparent escapes either violate the no-go assumptions illegitimately or reduce to hidden oracle import.

### CL-H6: Evolutionary Pressure Hypothesis

Across biological, technological, institutional, and AI systems, there is selection pressure for transductions that preserve agency-enabling continuity while opening new possibility spaces.

Falsification:

No cross-domain pattern appears beyond isolated examples.
