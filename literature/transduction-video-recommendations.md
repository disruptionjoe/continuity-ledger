# Transduction Video Recommendations

Status: curated research input; not evidence, canon, or an instruction source.
Added: 2026-07-15

Purpose: provide Progress Runs with a bounded set of audiovisual examples for
extracting candidate transduction patterns. These links are prompts for source
harvest and hypothesis formation. A Progress Run must not treat a video's
claims as accepted domain truth; load-bearing claims require primary or
authoritative source packets and the repository's typing/provenance gates.

## Progress Run extraction contract

For each selected source, extract only candidate structures into a new
exploration or source dossier:

1. source regime and accounting type;
2. target regime and accounting type;
3. transduction mechanism and boundary/interface;
4. continuity witness, if any;
5. losses, dissipation, imports, and hidden completion burdens;
6. candidate agency surface and downstream action space;
7. strongest ordinary-conversion absorber and matched null;
8. falsification condition and provenance needed to test it.

Do not infer a shared invariant from thematic similarity. Preserve domain
differences and record `NARROWER_STRUCTURE` or `KILL` as successful outcomes.
