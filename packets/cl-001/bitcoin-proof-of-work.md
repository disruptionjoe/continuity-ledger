# Bitcoin Proof-Of-Work Packet

## Construction Boundary

This packet will test whether Bitcoin proof-of-work can be represented as a
continuity-ledger transaction without treating electricity, hashwork,
settlement security, issuance eligibility, money, legitimacy, and agency as
one scalar.

No gate has been scored. This file does not assert that Bitcoin passes CL-001.

## Source Requirements

Before scoring, material claims need primary or protocol-level support for:

- the proof-of-work mechanism and chain selection rule;
- difficulty adjustment and block production;
- transaction ordering, settlement, and issuance behavior;
- participant roles and policy surfaces; and
- relevant energy, hardware, and network boundary imports.

The frozen WI-069 corpus may explain why this case was selected, but it is not
accepted evidence for packet fields.

## Absorber Pressure

- Ordinary payment network or accounting ledger.
- Energy expenditure with no agency-generating residue.
- Security protocol whose value is fully explained by existing participant
  preferences.
- Institutional coordination surface that imports legitimacy without recording
  it.

## Falsification Conditions

- The packet passes only by equating energy with money, legitimacy, security,
  or agency.
- The alleged agency surface is just an existing actor using a tool inside an
  unchanged action class.
- Settlement or issuance claims depend on hidden social, hardware, regulatory,
  or exchange-rate imports that are not recorded.
