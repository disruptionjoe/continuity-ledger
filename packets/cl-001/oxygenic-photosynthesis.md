# Oxygenic Photosynthesis Packet

## Construction Boundary

This packet will test whether oxygenic photosynthesis can be represented as a
continuity-ledger transaction without treating photons, chemical free energy,
biomass, ecological opportunity, and agency as one scalar.

No gate has been scored. This file does not assert that photosynthesis passes
CL-001.

## Source Requirements

Before scoring, material claims need primary scientific support for:

- light capture, electron transport, water splitting, and oxygen evolution;
- carbon fixation and biomass production boundaries;
- energy loss, heat, and biochemical efficiency limits;
- organismal and ecological agency surfaces; and
- environmental imports such as water, carbon dioxide, nutrients, and light.

The frozen WI-069 corpus may explain why this case was selected, but it is not
accepted evidence for packet fields.

- Biochemical mechanism with no agency-generating residue.
- Evolutionary adaptation inside an unchanged agent class.
- Ecological feedback fully explained without a continuity-ledger object.

## Falsification Conditions

- The packet passes only by equating photon energy with biomass, value,
  capability, or agency.
- The alleged agency surface is merely a human observer description.
- Ecological agency claims depend on unrecorded environmental imports or
  model-class changes.
