# Null Packet: Solar Heating Of Inert Matter

## Construction Boundary

This matched null tests whether solar heating of inert matter without a
self-maintaining biochemical or ecological agency loop can wrongly satisfy the
same gates as the photosynthesis target.

No gate has been scored. This file does not assert that the null fails or
passes CL-001.

## Source Requirements

Before scoring, material claims need evidence for the chosen null construction:

- solar energy input and heating behavior;
- absence of biochemical storage or self-maintaining metabolism;
- absence of generated or recruited ecological agency;
- loss and dissipation accounting; and
- the strongest ordinary-thermodynamic absorber.

The null must be scored with the same field and gate burden as the targets.

- The null is made to fail only by definitional exclusion.
- Hidden biochemical, ecological, or observer-side agency is smuggled into the
  null.
