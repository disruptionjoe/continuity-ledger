## Executive Research Object

Continuity Ledger studies regime-crossing transactions in which physical,
biological, computational, economic, institutional, or epistemic substrates
are converted into surfaces around which agents can orient, adapt, and create
new capabilities.

The repository asks whether such cases admit a rigorous typed ledger that
tracks what is transported, transformed, lost, imported, captured, or
compounded without pretending that unlike quantities are one conserved scalar.
It then asks whether the resulting agency can search beyond constraints that
bind the prior system class.

## Audacious North Star

Develop and force-or-falsify a theory of evolutionary agency escape:

> Open-ended systems persist by discovering or constructing transduction
> interfaces that preserve accountable continuity across changes of substrate
> and measurement, create new agency classes, and let those agents search
> possibility spaces outside prior class-relative limits. Collapse is not one
> universal event, but the typed failure, capture, exhaustion, or absorption of
> that recursive agency-generating capacity.

This is the highest-ceiling target, not a premise.

## Nearer Guiding Hypothesis

> A nontrivial family of cross-domain systems admits a typed continuity ledger
> that distinguishes agency-generating transduction from ordinary conversion,
> fixed-family disclosure, and observer-side relabeling by explicitly tracking
> source and target regimes, continuity witnesses, losses, boundary imports,
> generated agency, class-relative no-goes, escape moves, and recursive
> feedback.

This hypothesis is false or requires narrowing if:

- a common packet can describe radically different cases only by erasing their
  domain-specific types;
- the continuity relation reduces to vague causal storytelling;
- matched null controls satisfy the same gates as the target examples;
- every alleged escape is optimization inside the original class or depends on
  an unrecorded imported capability;
- the ledger does not yield a composition rule, discriminator, prediction,
  counterexample, or sharper impossibility result; or
- representation changes, quotienting, or absorber enlargement erase every
  proposed residue.

## Agentic Research Claim

The repository also tests a methodological claim:

> A governed system of AI agents can discover and pressure-test cross-domain
> theoretical objects by combining radically divergent case harvests, typed
> packetization, adversarial structural review, absorber search, matched
> controls, and explicit kill conditions, while preserving source-domain
> differences and negative results.

Success is not the production of a broad synthesis. Success is a reproducible
record that the agent system either found a nontrivial structure, narrowed it
to its valid domain, or killed it for clear reasons.

## Founding Motivation

WI-069 began with emergence and collapse prevention, then changed shape through
cross-domain examples. Photosynthesis does not merely disclose light; it
transduces photon energy into chemical free energy and biomass that sustains
plant growth and wider ecological agency. Bitcoin proof-of-work does not
literally conserve electricity as money; it converts energy expenditure,
hardware, protocol rules, and network time into chainwork, settlement security,
and issuance eligibility that can support a predictable monetary surface and
recruit human action.

The common candidate is neither energy nor value. It is the possibility of a
typed continuity relation across a transduction that creates an agency surface,
plus an explicit ledger for losses, imports, and downstream feedback.

The divergence is essential. If one framework can handle both examples only by
calling every causal chain a transaction, the program fails.

## Core Distinctions

### Transduction

A mechanism that changes the material substrate, accounting type, constraint
surface, or operational regime through which downstream action becomes
possible. Transduction is not automatically emergence.

### Continuity Ledger

A typed account of the relation between source and target regimes, including
provenance, conversion, preserved constraints or entitlements, losses,
externalities, boundary imports, and uncertainty. It need not be scalar,
additive, or conserved.

### Agency Surface

A sufficiently stable and action-relevant regularity, policy, gradient,
affordance, or expectation around which an agent class can adapt. Stability is
class- and timescale-relative.

### Agency Generation

The creation, recruitment, differentiation, or durable support of actors or
processes with a new action space. Merely changing an existing agent's output
does not establish agency generation.

### Class-Relative No-Go

An impossibility, closure, or obstruction proved or observed under a named
model class and assumptions. It is not refuted when a system changes class; the
ledger must record which assumption, substrate, interface, observer, or
admissibility rule changed.

### Escape

A constructive transition to a new class in which a previously unavailable
action, capability, or admissibility condition becomes available. Logical
non-exclusion is not a constructive escape.

### Collapse

A typed failure mode such as state convergence, loss of distinguishability,
fixed-completion factorization, agency exhaustion, capture, fragmentation,
source depletion, legitimacy loss, or failure to construct new admissibility.
No universal collapse scalar is presumed.

### Emergence

A candidate source-side enlargement of capability, agency, admissibility,
structure, or possibility relative to a specified class. Observer surprise,
finite-prefix novelty, new records, and new access are weaker and must be
separately labeled.

## Formal Spine

The first formal object is a typed transaction packet:

```text
CLTP = (R0, K0, S0, M0, T, B, R1, K1, S1, M1,
        C, L, I, P, A, Omega, N, X, F, Z, V)
```

where:

- `R0`, `R1` are source and target regimes;
- `K0`, `K1` are their model or admissibility classes;
- `S0`, `S1` are material or operational substrates;
- `M0`, `M1` are typed measurement/accounting families;
- `T` is the transduction mechanism and `B` its boundary/interface;
- `C` is a continuity witness, not presumed to be an equality;
- `L` records loss, dissipation, degradation, and uncertainty;
- `I` records external imports, subsidies, or hidden completion burdens;
- `P` is the candidate agency surface;
- `A` and `Omega` are the generated or recruited agent class and action space;
- `N` is the class-relative no-go or constraint ledger;
- `X` is the constructive escape witness and changed-assumption ledger;
- `F` is downstream feedback into future regimes;
- `Z` is the strongest absorber or null model; and
- `V` is the evidence and provenance record.

The tuple is provisional. Its fields may split, compose, or die under testing.

### Matched Nulls

Nulls carry two different evidentiary weights and must not be confused.

**Floor nulls** confirm that the packet rejects the trivial case. They are
specified by stipulated absence of an agency surface, so their failure at the
agency gate follows from the specification rather than from evidence. They
carry **zero weight** toward any positive verdict:

- energy-consuming hash computation with no persistent consensus, settlement,
  or participant policy surface;
- solar heating of inert matter without a self-maintaining biochemical or
  ecological agency loop.

**Near-miss nulls** are the load-bearing controls. Each must be a named real
system, specified positively by what it *is*, described without reference to
the target's success criterion, and selected because it might genuinely pass.
Its gate verdicts are then findings rather than tautologies:

- Bitcoin testnet or a regtest chain: full consensus, settlement, issuance,
  participant ecology, and proof-of-work, with economically worthless coins.
- Rayleigh-Benard convection or a solar pond: real dissipative structure,
  self-organization, and far-from-equilibrium ordering, with no metabolism and
  no heredity.

A near-miss null may never be selected on the grounds that it lacks an agency
surface. Selecting for absence relocates the stipulation from specification
time to selection time and reproduces the defect this distinction exists to
remove. If a candidate is admissible only by stipulating an absence, it is a
floor null. If it turns out to carry a durable recognition surface, that is a
finding about the target's discriminator, not grounds for discarding the null.

If no admissible near-miss null can be constructed for a target, the correct
verdict is `KILL` on grounds of uncontrollability. That is a successful result.

### Required Gates

1. **Typing gate** - unlike quantities remain unlike.
2. **Provenance gate** - material claims trace to primary domain evidence.
3. **Transduction gate** - an operative conversion mechanism is identified.
4. **Continuity gate** - the claimed relation is explicit and testable.
5. **Loss/import gate** - dissipation, externalities, subsidies, and hidden
   structure are recorded.
6. **Agency gate** - a new or durably recruited action class is demonstrated.
7. **No-go/escape gate** - the old class, assumptions, and changed class are
   explicit.
8. **Feedback gate** - second- or third-order action changes future conditions.
9. **Absorber gate** - ordinary conversion, control theory, economics,
   thermodynamics, evolution, and fixed-completion explanations are tested.
10. **Neutrality gate** - the result does not depend on preferring openness,
    Bitcoin, biological life, or emergence.

The allowed verdicts are `SHARED_STRUCTURE`, `NARROWER_STRUCTURE`, and `KILL`.

## Representation And Conservation Discipline

- A continuity witness must state its covariance or transformation behavior.
- A scalar comparison requires compatible types and units or an explicit
  conversion map.
- Monetary value is not physical energy; legitimacy is not information;
  capability is not entropy.
- Non-factorization is relative to a declared absorber class.
- A proposed invariant must survive admissible renaming, coordinate change,
  quotienting, observer change, and completion enlargement relevant to its
  model class.
- A ledger that merely records everything after the fact is not explanatory.

## Closure Neutrality

This repository does not presume openness is good or closure is collapse.
Finality, canonicalization, equilibrium, and constraint can enable agency and
coherence. The scientific question is which closure is productive, terminal,
captured, or irrelevant under a specified objective and model class.

## Success Standard

The program succeeds if it produces at least one of:

- a nontrivial typed structure that predicts or forbids something across
  multiple domains;
- a composition or non-composition theorem for continuity/escape ledgers;
- a useful classifier that rejects matched nulls;
- a sharp no-go showing why a universal emergence invariant cannot exist; or
- a reproducible agentic method that discovers, narrows, or kills cross-domain
  theoretical objects more rigorously than thematic synthesis.

A clean kill is a successful result.
