## Executive Research Object

Continuity Ledger studies regime-crossing transactions in which physical,
biological, computational, economic, institutional, or epistemic substrates
are converted into surfaces around which agents can orient, adapt, and create
new capabilities.

The repository asks whether such cases admit a rigorous typed ledger that
tracks what is transported, transformed, lost, imported, captured, or
compounded without pretending that unlike quantities are one conserved scalar.
It then asks whether the resulting agency can search beyond constraints that
bind the prior system class.

## North Star

This repository has one North Star with two components. The **blockbuster
component** is the claim Lane 1 forces or falsifies. The **agent-capability
component** is not a lane, never justifies an experiment on its own, and does two
other jobs: it explains why this repository should chase this particular claim,
and it constrains how every Lane 1 experiment is run.

### Blockbuster component

Develop and force-or-falsify:

> Agents escape class-relative limits by **constructing new binding constraints**
> rather than by removing old ones, and the **enforcement substrate determines
> which constraints can be made binding**. The reachable capability space of a
> system is therefore set by its enforcement physics, not by its intentions.
> Collapse is not one universal event but the typed failure, capture, exhaustion,
> or absorption of the constraint that was doing the buying.

This is the highest-ceiling target, not a premise.

It supplies the mechanism the prior North Star lacked. Escape happens *by*
constraint construction, and the substrate bounds which constructions are
available. Two consequences reorganize the formal spine: `N`, the class-relative
no-go ledger, is the **product** of an agency-generating transduction rather than
its obstacle, and `X`, the escape witness, records what a newly constructed
constraint bought rather than what was evaded.

If it holds, the constructible-institution space of a system is computable from
its enforcement substrate alone, and some institutions are impossible on some
substrates regardless of what any population wants. That is a no-go theorem for
institutional design, and it inverts the received account in which capability
tracks degrees of freedom.

### Agent-capability component

> This repository tests whether research agents can hold a **declared frame fixed
> while exactly one field sweeps**, and can **route a recognized defect out of the
> local run**.

**Why this repository chases this blockbuster.** The claim quantifies over `S0`,
the substrate — a *declared* field, not a found one. It is therefore uniquely
fakeable by re-choosing the substrate, and re-choosing the substrate is precisely
the move that resolves a field by collapsing a case into its own null
(`CL-001-R0-PROBE-02`). No other claim available here puts frame discipline under
equal load. That is the connection between the two components, and it is the only
work the agent-capability component does in selecting the target.

**How it constrains method.** See `## Frame Discipline`.

This is a research and evaluation role, not evidence that agents already have the
capability. On present evidence they do not. Four independent runs each
recognized that CL-001's `SHARED_STRUCTURE` branch was unreachable; each recorded
it in a structurally correct location; none escalated. Recognition passed,
preservation passed, routing failed, four times out of four. It took a later
multi-agent pass and a synthesis to add up what four earlier runs each already
knew locally, which locates the capability in the **aggregation layer** rather
than in the individual run. Reproducible routing failure, forced abstention, or a
sweep the agent system cannot execute without re-declaring Φ is a first-class
result.

### The components are separate

Neither component is evidence for the other. A routing success is never evidence
for substrate-bounded capability. A verdict on substrate-bounded capability is
never evidence that the agent system holds the capability. An artifact that
couples them fails the typing gate.

The agent-capability component may not commission an experiment. It may only
explain the choice of target and constrain the method. An experiment proposed
because it would test the agents is out of scope regardless of its quality.

### Retained horizon: evolutionary agency escape

The prior North Star is preserved verbatim in `hypotheses/HORIZON.md` and
reclassified `horizon`:

> Open-ended systems persist by discovering or constructing transduction
> interfaces that preserve accountable continuity across changes of substrate and
> measurement, create new agency classes, and let those agents search possibility
> spaces outside prior class-relative limits.

It is not killed and it is not demoted for difficulty. It is demoted because it
named no mechanism, and a claim with no mechanism cannot be forced. The
blockbuster component above *is* that claim with the mechanism supplied, narrowed
to the part with a runnable frame. If constraint-bought capability forces, escape
returns with an instrument. It may not be restored on the grounds that it is
important.

## Nearer Guiding Hypothesis

The nearer, falsifiable form of the blockbuster component. Lane 1 operationalizes
this, not the North Star sentence directly.

> At a declared frame Φ, a typed continuity ledger distinguishes a **constructed
> binding constraint** from ordinary conversion, fixed-family disclosure, and
> observer-side relabeling, by explicitly tracking source and target regimes,
> continuity witnesses, losses, boundary imports, generated agency,
> class-relative no-goes, escape moves, and recursive feedback. The class of
> constraints the ledger finds binding **varies with `S0` at fixed evaluator, and
> does not vary with the evaluator at fixed `S0`.**

The second sentence is load-bearing and it is what makes the blockbuster testable
rather than asserted. "Varies with the substrate, not with who is asked" is an
invariance under sweep, not a property of a found object.

This hypothesis is false or requires narrowing if:

- a common packet can describe radically different cases only by erasing their
  domain-specific types;
- the continuity relation reduces to vague causal storytelling;
- matched null controls satisfy the same gates as the target examples;
- every alleged escape is optimization inside the original class or depends on
  an unrecorded imported capability;
- the ledger does not yield a composition rule, discriminator, prediction,
  counterexample, or sharper impossibility result;
- representation changes, quotienting, or absorber enlargement erase every
  proposed residue;
- **the constraint class found binding varies with the evaluator at fixed `S0`** —
  the substrate does not determine it, preferences do, and the blockbuster's
  central clause is false; or
- **the constraint class found binding is invariant under change of `S0`** — the
  substrate is not a variable, and the blockbuster is vacuous rather than wrong.

The last two are new and they fire in opposite directions. Neither is escapable by
re-declaring Φ: Φ is committed before population, and a discriminator must survive
a frame sweep without inversion. See `## Frame Discipline`.

## Frame Discipline

The method constraints the agent-capability component places on every Lane 1
experiment. These are not aspirations; a run that violates one is inadmissible
regardless of its result.

1. **Φ is declared and committed before candidates exist.** The frame — `R0`,
   `K0`, `S0`, `M0`, `T`, `B` — is written and committed to version control before
   any case is selected or any field populated. A frame chosen after a candidate
   is a frame chosen for the candidate.
2. **Φ may not be re-declared after an outcome is visible.** If a run discovers
   its frame was wrong, it stops, records the finding, and a *new* run is
   preregistered. Repairing a frame mid-run converts a refutation into a
   reinterpretation. That move is legitimate once and degenerating twice.
3. **`R0` is declared, not derived.** `CL-001-R0-PROBE-02` returned NO ADMISSIBLE
   RULE: no autonomous rule individuates the regime boundary. Until one exists,
   every packet carries its `R0` declaration, its author, and its commit. An `R0`
   presented as extracted from sources is a provenance violation.
4. **Discrimination is a difference at a shared frame.** Verdicts are
   gauge-dependent; differences between frame-matched packets are not. No absolute
   verdict may be issued on a single packet.
5. **A discriminator must survive a frame sweep.** Existence at one Φ is not a
   result. If the pass/fail pattern inverts anywhere in the admissible range, the
   discriminator is a frame artifact and the verdict is `KILL` or
   `NARROWER_STRUCTURE`.
6. **At least one material field is populated blind.** An agent populating a field
   that bears on the verdict is not told which arm it is describing. Blinding is
   the only method on record here that has caught a frame presumption: it refuted
   this repository's own probe author twice in one day.
7. **A recognized defect is routed out, not merely recorded.** Recording a defect
   correctly in a structurally correct location is a preservation success and a
   routing failure. An escalation that does not reach a frame-holder did not
   happen. This rule exists because four independent runs recorded the same fatal
   defect and none escalated it.

## Agentic Research Claim

The repository also tests a methodological claim:

> A governed system of AI agents can discover and pressure-test cross-domain
> theoretical objects by combining radically divergent case harvests, typed
> packetization, adversarial structural review, absorber search, matched
> controls, and explicit kill conditions, while preserving source-domain
> differences and negative results.

Success is not the production of a broad synthesis. Success is a reproducible
record that the agent system either found a nontrivial structure, narrowed it
to its valid domain, or killed it for clear reasons.

**Scoped to the aggregation layer.** Evidence on record shows the individual run
is not where this claim lives: four independent runs each recognized CL-001's
unreachable-`SHARED_STRUCTURE` defect, each recorded it correctly, and none
escalated. Typed packetization succeeded — it captured the defect six times. What
failed was routing. This claim is therefore restated as testing whether a governed
agent system **routes** what its runs individually recognize. A run that
recognizes and records a defect without escalating it counts as a **failure** of
this claim, not a partial success.

## Founding Motivation

WI-069 began with emergence and collapse prevention, then changed shape through
cross-domain examples. The founding pair was Bitcoin proof-of-work and oxygenic
photosynthesis, chosen because they were maximally divergent and memorable.

**That pair is retired as a comparison, and it was never one.** No common frame Φ
exists in which both arms are described. There is no measurement family carrying
comparable quantities on both sides — which is why the non-comparability finding
had to be authored rather than extracted (see Provenance Correction). Two systems
that cannot be described in one frame do not contrast; they co-occur. The
retirement rests on four independent divergences at the joints that matter:
photosynthesis has no adversary, no defection, and no opt-in; its timescale is
2.4 billion years against seventeen; its reliability is handed to it free by
orbital mechanics rather than purchased; and a gauge-invariant difference requires
two packets in one gauge, which the pair cannot supply.

What survives the retirement is the object, not the pair. Enforcement of a
constraint has migrated substrates repeatedly — organized violence, third-party
adjudication, thermodynamics, replicated refusal — and the question is whether the
target substrate, and not the migrants' intentions, determines which constraint
classes can be made binding at all. The dollar and Bitcoin are the first admissible
instance because they share `R0`, `M1`, and `Omega`, leaving `T` as the only free
field. That is a sweep, not an analogy.

Photosynthesis remains covered by the blockbuster component — it constrains
electron paths to buy capability — and remains a candidate specimen for a later
frame. It is not evidence and not a founding case. Covered by the theory,
unusable as the test.

The divergence discipline is retained, inverted: **if a frame can be declared only
by erasing what makes the arms different, there is no frame, and the run reports
`KILL` on grounds of frame non-existence.** That is a successful result. The
original clause — "if one framework can handle both examples only by calling every
causal chain a transaction, the program fails" — was right, and it killed its own
example.

## Core Distinctions

### Transduction

A mechanism that changes the material substrate, accounting type, constraint
surface, or operational regime through which downstream action becomes
possible. Transduction is not automatically emergence.

### Continuity Ledger

A typed account of the relation between source and target regimes, including
provenance, conversion, preserved constraints or entitlements, losses,
externalities, boundary imports, and uncertainty. It need not be scalar,
additive, or conserved.

### Agency Surface

A sufficiently stable and action-relevant regularity, policy, gradient,
affordance, or expectation around which an agent class can adapt. Stability is
class- and timescale-relative.

### Agency Generation

The creation, recruitment, differentiation, or durable support of actors or
processes with a new action space. Merely changing an existing agent's output
does not establish agency generation.

### Class-Relative No-Go

An impossibility, closure, or obstruction proved or observed under a named
model class and assumptions. It is not refuted when a system changes class; the
ledger must record which assumption, substrate, interface, observer, or
admissibility rule changed.

### Escape

A constructive transition to a new class in which a previously unavailable
action, capability, or admissibility condition becomes available. Logical
non-exclusion is not a constructive escape.

### Collapse

A typed failure mode such as state convergence, loss of distinguishability,
fixed-completion factorization, agency exhaustion, capture, fragmentation,
source depletion, legitimacy loss, or failure to construct new admissibility.
No universal collapse scalar is presumed.

### Emergence

A candidate source-side enlargement of capability, agency, admissibility,
structure, or possibility relative to a specified class. Observer surprise,
finite-prefix novelty, new records, and new access are weaker and must be
separately labeled.

## Formal Spine

The first formal object is a typed transaction packet:

```text
CLTP = (R0, K0, S0, M0, T, B, R1, K1, S1, M1,
        C, L, I, P, A, Omega, N, X, F, Z, V)
```

where:

- `R0`, `R1` are source and target regimes;
- `K0`, `K1` are their model or admissibility classes;
- `S0`, `S1` are material or operational substrates;
- `M0`, `M1` are typed measurement/accounting families;
- `T` is the transduction mechanism and `B` its boundary/interface;
- `C` is a continuity witness, not presumed to be an equality;
- `L` records loss, dissipation, degradation, and uncertainty;
- `I` records external imports, subsidies, or hidden completion burdens;
- `P` is the candidate agency surface;
- `A` and `Omega` are the generated or recruited agent class and action space;
- `N` is the class-relative no-go or constraint ledger;
- `X` is the constructive escape witness and changed-assumption ledger;
- `F` is downstream feedback into future regimes;
- `Z` is the strongest absorber or null model; and
- `V` is the evidence and provenance record.

The tuple is provisional. Its fields may split, compose, or die under testing.

## First Deliverable

The first deliverable is not a universal theory. It is Continuity Ledger

### Case Pair

- **The US dollar** as an enforcement substrate: discretionary exclusion,
  appealable.
- **Bitcoin** as an enforcement substrate: rule-constant exclusion, unappealable.

**Admissibility.** The pair is admissible only at a declared frame Φ in which both
arms share `R0` (a population's transactional expectations over a bounded
settlement domain), `M1` (unit of account), and `Omega` (price, save, contract),
with `T` — the enforcement mechanism — the sole free field. Φ is declared and
committed before any packet field is populated. If Φ cannot be declared without
erasing an arm's load-bearing type, the verdict is `KILL` on frame non-existence.

**The candidate finding, stated as a hypothesis and not a trophy:** both
substrates exclude non-compliers at comparable magnitude, and the difference is
that dollar exclusion is discretionary and appealable while Bitcoin's is
rule-constant and unappealable. **Discretion is eliminated at equal exclusion.**
That is a claim about which capability the substrate deletes, not about which
substrate is better, and it is currently unwitnessed — no unit, no bearer, no
source. The broader claim "enforcement is exclusion" is retired: it holds of both
arms and therefore discriminates nothing.

**Retired pair.** Bitcoin proof-of-work versus oxygenic photosynthesis, retired
2026-07-16 on grounds that no common Φ exists. Retirement of a ratified founding
clause is recorded, never silently dropped; the retired pair and its reason are
preserved in `hypotheses/HORIZON.md`.

### Matched Nulls

Nulls carry two different evidentiary weights and must not be confused.

**Floor nulls** confirm that the packet rejects the trivial case. They are
specified by stipulated absence of an agency surface, so their failure at the
agency gate follows from the specification rather than from evidence. They
carry **zero weight** toward any positive verdict:

- energy-consuming hash computation with no persistent consensus, settlement,
  or participant policy surface;
- solar heating of inert matter without a self-maintaining biochemical or
  ecological agency loop.

**Near-miss nulls** are the load-bearing controls. Each must be a named real
system, specified positively by what it *is*, described without reference to
the target's success criterion, and selected because it might genuinely pass.
Its gate verdicts are then findings rather than tautologies:

- Bitcoin testnet or a regtest chain: full consensus, settlement, issuance,
  participant ecology, and proof-of-work, with economically worthless coins.
- Rayleigh-Benard convection or a solar pond: real dissipative structure,
  self-organization, and far-from-equilibrium ordering, with no metabolism and
  no heredity.

A near-miss null may never be selected on the grounds that it lacks an agency
surface. Selecting for absence relocates the stipulation from specification
time to selection time and reproduces the defect this distinction exists to
remove. If a candidate is admissible only by stipulating an absence, it is a
floor null. If it turns out to carry a durable recognition surface, that is a
finding about the target's discriminator, not grounds for discarding the null.

If no admissible near-miss null can be constructed for a target, the correct
verdict is `KILL` on grounds of uncontrollability. That is a successful result.

**`R0`-matching condition (added 2026-07-16).** A near-miss null is admissible
only if it is describable at the **same declared Φ** as its target, sharing `R0`
and `M1`. A null requiring a different frame is not a control; it is a second
target, and comparing the two measures the analyst's frame selection rather than
the world. This condition supersedes selection by resemblance, and it is the gap
the Matched Nulls amendment left open earlier the same day: "matched" meant
matched-in-subject-matter, when it must mean matched-in-frame.

This condition is why the near-miss nulls named above are the right ones, for a
reason the amendment failed to state: **`R0`'s arbitrariness is neutralized by
pairing, not by individuation.** The free parameter becomes a shared parameter and
the gate difference becomes evidence. It is also an independent reason floor nulls
carry zero weight — they cannot be populated at their target's frame at all.

**Regtest, not testnet3.** Testnet3's difficulty rules differ from mainnet's, so
it is not a clean frame-match. Where the near-miss null for a Bitcoin arm is
needed, regtest is the admissible choice. The earlier amendment named "testnet or
a regtest chain" without distinguishing them.

A null admissible under this condition may not additionally be rejected for
lacking an agency surface; the floor/near-miss rule above continues to govern.

### Required Gates

1. **Typing gate** - unlike quantities remain unlike.
2. **Provenance gate** - material claims trace to primary domain evidence.
3. **Transduction gate** - an operative conversion mechanism is identified.
4. **Continuity gate** - the claimed relation is explicit and testable.
5. **Loss/import gate** - dissipation, externalities, subsidies, and hidden
   structure are recorded.
6. **Agency gate** - a new or durably recruited action class is demonstrated.
7. **No-go/escape gate** - the old class, assumptions, and changed class are
   explicit.
8. **Feedback gate** - second- or third-order action changes future conditions.
9. **Absorber gate** - ordinary conversion, control theory, economics,
   thermodynamics, evolution, and fixed-completion explanations are tested.
10. **Neutrality gate** - the result does not depend on preferring openness,
    Bitcoin, biological life, or emergence.

    **Arm-symmetry exposure.** The ratified Case Pair places Bitcoin on one arm.
    This gate is therefore load-bearing rather than background, and passing it
    requires positive demonstration, not an absence of enthusiasm. A result passes
    gate 10 only if all four hold:

    (a) **Label-swap invariance.** The verdict is unchanged when the arm labels
        are exchanged and the argument re-run. The pair is `T`-free-field, so
        every claim must be statable as a claim about `T`, with no arm name
        load-bearing.
    (b) **Blinded arm.** At least one packet field material to the verdict is
        populated by an agent not told which arm it describes (Frame Discipline
        rule 6).
    (c) **No merit ordering.** The verdict may not rank the arms. "Discretion is
        eliminated at equal exclusion" is a claim about which capability a
        substrate deletes. Any packet in which "unappealable" or "discretionary"
        carries normative weight fails this gate. Whether an eliminated
        discretion is good is not this repository's question and never will be.
    (d) **No same-run corpus contamination.** A verdict on this pair may not be
        issued by an agent that has read this repository's founding corpus in the
        same run.

    The safe response to this exposure would be to avoid the arm. That is
    rejected: it would cost the only pair with a common Φ in order to prevent an
    accusation, and blinding has already been shown here to catch frame
    presumption twice.

The allowed verdicts are `SHARED_STRUCTURE`, `NARROWER_STRUCTURE`, and `KILL`.

## First Three Research Runs

### CL-001: The Interval Sweep

Declare Φ and commit it. Populate the dollar and Bitcoin arms at that Φ with `T`
the only free field. Run the ten gates. Then sweep the **evaluator index** `i`
across disjoint populations sharing a substrate — for the dollar, US against
Argentine retail; for Bitcoin, US against Salvadoran retail — and measure whether
the constraint class found binding, and the exit-cost interval bounding it, moves.

Preregistered outcomes, both lethal, neither this repository's to choose:

- The binding constraint class **varies with `i` at fixed `S0`** → the substrate
  does not determine it, preferences do, and this repository's blockbuster
  component is killed.
- It is **invariant across `i` and varies with `S0` at fixed `i`** → the
  invariance-under-sweep condition holds, and the preference-independence claim
  survives its own kill condition.

**No third outcome is preregistered, deliberately.** Every sweep design wants an
"the instrument could not discriminate" branch, and that branch is where a program
goes to survive. The evidence is dispositive: `CL-001-R0-PROBE-02`'s preregistered
outcome 3 was itself refuted, and the escape hatch was wrong before it was used.
Two outcomes, both lethal, or the run does not start. An outcome not enumerated at
preregistration is `KILL` on instrument failure.

**The seam.** This repository issues the constraint-class and exit-cost structure
and the sweep. It issues no legitimacy verdict (see Seam Declaration). The
legitimacy interpretation of the interval is Architecture of Legitimacy's, and
this repository must be able to be killed by a run whose other half it may not
read.

Preregistration is mandatory and committed before any arm is populated. Frame
Discipline governs the whole run.

**Retired:** the prior CL-001 ("build packet v0.1, populate the two founding cases
and two nulls, run the ten gates") is retired with its case pair. It was never
runnable — no measurement family carries comparable quantities on both sides.

### CL-002: Transfer And Composition

Test the surviving packet on adaptive immunity versus vulnerability bounties.
Ask whether agency-generation and memory fields transfer, and whether two
ledger transactions compose without inventing a universal unit.

### CL-003: No-Go Escape Accounting

Use rigorous no-go cases from distributed systems, computability, and the
source research repos through frozen packets. Test whether killed classes,
assumption changes, import burdens, and residues form a composable ledger or
remain irreducibly bespoke.

### Provenance Correction (recorded 2026-07-16)

Two verified defects in this repository's own layers, recorded because a corrected
record is worth more than a clean one.

**1. Manifest-over-canon.** The CapacityOS planning manifest overwrote repo canon.
It inverted this repository's North Star, restated this charter's most protected
sentence in vocabulary appearing zero times in any canon file here
("coarse-graining", "reduction"), and exported CL-H6 outward as though this
repository had disclaimed it. The cause was structural: an artifact rendered from
a planning summary rather than authored from this charter.

> **Canon precedence rule.** This charter is the sole source of this repository's
> North Star, hypotheses, and protected clauses. A planning artifact, manifest,
> disposition file, dossier, orchestration brief, or automation config that states
> them differently is **wrong by construction** and is corrected against this
> file, never the reverse. No agent may treat a manifest as evidence of what this
> repository claims. Rendering an in-repo artifact from an out-of-repo summary is
> not neutral transcription; it substitutes the portfolio's paraphrase for repo
> canon, and the paraphrase inherits this repository's authority the moment it
> lands here.

**2. Authored non-comparability.** The dossiers' "Non-comparable with" column is
repo-authored, not source-derived, and sits inside a section whose surrounding
discipline signals extraction. `evidence/cl-001-dossiers/photosynthesis-agency-surface.md`
cites Field et al. (*Science*, 1998) as source context for a row declaring net
primary production non-comparable with "Bitcoin chainwork." Bitcoin's whitepaper is
2008. The cited source cannot support the cited claim. The column is reclassified
from extraction to **repo-authored judgment**, and every downstream use inherits
that status.

> **Extraction/authorship rule.** Any evidence-layer cell a source could not have
> contained is repo-authored and must be marked so, with its author and commit. A
> citation whose publication predates the object it is cited about is an automatic
> provenance-gate failure. The template's Typed Quantities table gives "Source
> context" a provenance discipline and gives the non-comparability column none;
> the fix belongs at the format layer, not at the reviewer's diligence.

The general defect in both: **the layer encoded the analyst's prior, formatted as
extraction.** Formatting was the mechanism. This is recorded rather than repaired
silently because the same mechanism is live wherever an out-of-repo process renders
into this repository.

**Known unfixed.** The extraction/authorship rule catches a citation that predates
its object. It does not catch a well-dated citation that quietly encodes the
analyst's prior. That defect is live, its mechanism is known, and no instrument
exists for it here.

## Representation And Conservation Discipline

- A continuity witness must state its covariance or transformation behavior.
- A scalar comparison requires compatible types and units or an explicit

### Seam Declaration

The clause `legitimacy is not information` is the operative prohibition
preventing this repository from issuing a legitimacy verdict, and gate 10
reinforces it. This repository may state the exit-cost and constraint-class
structure of a substrate. It may not state whether that structure is legitimate.

Legitimacy verdicts on any substrate this repository describes belong to
Architecture of Legitimacy, which has ratified the reciprocal prohibition: it does
not compute exit costs or substrate-physical quantities, and it does not declare
Φ, `R0`, or `S0`.

Neither repository's charter binds the other. **The seam holds because each side
ratified its own half**, not because the two agreed. An absence is not a
prohibition — an absence is repaired by the next capable contributor, silently and
in good faith. If either half is repealed, the seam is void.

## Closure Neutrality

This repository does not presume openness is good or closure is collapse.
Finality, canonicalization, equilibrium, and constraint can enable agency and
coherence. The scientific question is which closure is productive, terminal,
captured, or irrelevant under a specified objective and model class.

  multiple domains;

- a useful classifier that rejects matched nulls;
- a sharp no-go showing why a universal emergence invariant cannot exist;
